The Best Movies Ever!!!

According to Adeline Carpenter

Howl's Moving Castle

A really good, family-friendly movie. It has great aesthetics and a very cute story.

Crimson Peak

A creepy movie. It has amazing gothic visuals and my favorite actor.

Gran Turismo

An awesome movie about racing. The driving sequences are phenomenal.

Pride and Prejudice (2005)

A classic movie that everyone should watch. It is truly timeless.
